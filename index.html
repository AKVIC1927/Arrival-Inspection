Truck Anatomy

Front of the Car

Front Bumper

  • Check for broken plastic.
  • Look for items screwed into the bumper.
  • Front Valance:
  • Verify if missing or broken.

Grille

  • Check for cracks.
  • Look for chips.
  • Headlights:
  • Inspect general condition: Verify if missing or broken.

Windshield

  • Check both inside and outside surfaces for damage.

Interior

Door Interior

  • Inspect the step for damage, scratches, or rust.

Driver's Seat

  • Pay special attention to wear, particularly on the sides.

Side of the Car

Fender

  • Inspect for overall condition.
  • Ensure it's not an aftermarket plastic replacement.

Mirrors

  • Verify they are not cracked or broken.
  • Ensure they are movable; check for potential breakage.

Side Step

  • Inspect the step for damage, scratches, or rust.

Quarter Panel

  • Look for sandblasting damage from big wheels.
  • Check for scratches and chips.

Mudflaps

  • Check for proper installation.
  • Look for holes where they might be secured.

Roof

  • Inspect for scratches or holes.

Rear of the Car

Rear Bumper

  • Inspect for usual damage such as scratches, missing lights, and dents.
  • Taillights:
  • Look for usual damage and check functionality.
  • Tailgate:
  • Check for common damages. Ensure it opens properly. Inspect handles for scratches.

Miscellaneous

Frame

  • Check the front and back frames for any rust.

Aftermarket

  • Remove all aftermarket lights, or addons.
  • FOR AN OFFROADER: We will keep those addons (if in good condition). People want those. On a normal F150 or other Pickup, we will remove them.

License Plate

  • Ensure removal of the license plate.

Aftermarket Stickers

  • Remove any aftermarket stickers.
